This is a Kaldi recipe for the 6th CHiME Speech Separation and Recognition Challenge (CHiME-6).

See http://spandh.dcs.shef.ac.uk/chime_challenge/ for more detailed information.

The following recipes are provided:

s5_track1 : Track 1 of the challenge baseline (oracle segments and speaker label is provided)
s5_track2 : Track 2 of the challenge baseline (only raw audio is provided)
s5b_track1: Track 1 of the JHU-CLSP submission to the challenge
s5b_track2: Track 2 of the JHU-CLSP submission to the challenge
s5c_track2: Track 2 with TS-VAD based diarization
